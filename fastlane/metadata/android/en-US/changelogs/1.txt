Initial release of VeloSpot.

• 100,000+ bike parking spots from OpenStreetMap covering all of Germany – offline from first launch
• Interactive vector map with MapLibre (no API key, no tracking)
• Offline routing with BRouter (5 cycling profiles) and online fallback via OSRM
• Address search with live suggestions (Nominatim)
• Drop a custom pin anywhere on the map and navigate directly
• Favourites, parking photos, dark mode, 8 languages
• No proprietary Google services, no ads, no tracking
